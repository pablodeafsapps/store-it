/speckit.tasks

Once done, use the Trello MCP with the provided credentials to create a new board called "Store it!" and populate it with the recently created tasks to have total control of the development process. Assign tasks with a priority and a deadline, taking into account that a Senior Android Engineer will be working on the project 15/20 hours a week. Bear in mind the Spanish calendar to consider bank holidays and festivities.
